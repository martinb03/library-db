	Borrowings: filter by date range, active/overdue, customer

	Overdue Borrowings: list with customer details and due dates

	Books by Condition/Status: counts and lists (e.g., all damaged books)

	Penalties Report: penalties issued per customer or period

	Borrowing History: full history for a specific customer
